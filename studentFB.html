    MATH 102 Spring 2024 (Instructor of Record)

    Student Feedback
  • Pro: "Prof. Podariu is always welcoming to questions and helping anyone who needs it, in and out of the classroom. In class, we're given many opportunities to ask questions one-on-one and in lecture settings. When I do my homework outside of class and I'm not sure what to do on a problem, I know I can email her and get a helpful, timely response."
  • Con: "I know there's not much one can do with a pre-decided math program to have students learn from and not very flexible curriculum, but I feel there could be improvement in ways of explaining concepts and helping students really absorb the information. I took a college Trigonometry class in high school, so it was a lot easier for me to pick stuff up, as a lot of it was either just review or a slight extension from concepts I was already familiar with(though not the easiest sometimes with little explanation); I don't see the most successful track for students who are new to Trig, as most are who take this class. Simply giving more explanations for more complicated concepts, and maybe some notes or advice that made this easier for Prof. Podariu to learn these concepts when she did could make a big difference in students' ability to learn and apply the material. Overall, I found the materials adequate, but I would've appreciated a few options other than sometimes unclear explanations on concepts given in notes."
